Education

General health information — not a substitute for clinical care.

STIs

Testing & prevention

Many STIs have no symptoms. Regular testing helps you stay informed. Condoms and dental dams reduce risk when used correctly every time.

Communication

Talking with partners

Open conversations about boundaries, testing history, and protection can feel awkward — and they still matter. Pick a calm moment and use language that feels true to you.

Consent

Healthy relationships

Consent is ongoing, specific, and reversible. You can change your mind at any time. Trusted adults and counselors can help if something feels off.

Next steps

When to see a clinician

New symptoms, partner diagnosis, or routine screening are all good reasons to visit a clinic. Use Health Locator to find a site near you.
